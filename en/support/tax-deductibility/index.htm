United States

WorldWide CDI Organization is a non-profit organization (tax ID 20-0048704) incorporated in the United States under Section 501 (c) (3) of the IRS Code Section 501 (c) (3). entities located in the United States may not be taxed.

Canada

Due to the WorldWide CDI Organization's 501 (c) 3 status in the United States, the Canadian tax code generally allows for the deduction of donations to the WorldWide CDI Organization, but only from the US sponsor's taxable income. You may find it helpful to refer to Article XXI, paragraph 6, of the US-Canada Tax Treaty. Please contact your tax professional for details on deducting such a donation.

France

Donations to the WorldWide CDI Organization are tax-free in France.

Nederlands

The Dutch tax authorities have confirmed that, under Dutch law, the WorldWide CDI Organization qualifies as a “public welfare institution” (in Dutch “algemeen nut beogende instelling” or “ANBI”). This classification means that if you are a resident of the Netherlands, any donations made to the WorldWide CDI Organization are deducted from your income tax in accordance with the usual Dutch tax rules governing the deduction of donations.

Other countries

The WorldWide CDI Organization is a non-profit organization established in the United States under section 501 (c) (3) of the United States Internal Revenue Service, and donations from individuals or businesses located in the United States may not be taxed. Donations from individuals or businesses located outside the United States may not be eligible for tax deductions in the United States and elsewhere. In such cases, sponsors should seek local tax advice. It is important to note that WWCO does not ask for donations from individuals or legal entities located in any jurisdiction that prohibits or restricts fundraising activities by international charitable organizations, such as Organizations around the world, or collects taxes on donations made to such international charities.

The WorldWide CDI Organization supports local independent organizations around the world.
