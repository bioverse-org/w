About us

Our goal is to ensure the pursuit of free knowledge.

The WorldWide CDI Organization is a non-profit organization for online education, the main function of which is educational content and its global distribution. The organization maintains the infrastructure for the work of many scientific projects, including projects based on wiki principles: Atom, Andromeda, Andromeda Earth, Mediatop, Media, GEL'S.

Symbols of WWCO

 The symbols of WWCO - the official symbols of the organization since 2019.

Since The WorldWide CDI Organization was founded, its symbolism has changed several times, this was due to the fact that we could not more accurately determine its name and exact direction, but now, after 2.5 years, our organization has found what it was looking for.
