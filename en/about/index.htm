About us

Our goal is to ensure the pursuit of free knowledge.

The WorldWide CDI Organization is a non-profit online education organization whose main function lies in educational content and its global distribution. The organization supports the infrastructure of many scientific projects, including projects based on wiki principles: Atom, Andromeda, Andromeda Earth, Mediatop, Media, GEL'S.

More about us

WWCO Projects

More than 50 million people support and complement WWCO projects. The most popular encyclopedia on nuclear energy "Atom " - founded the list of all nuclear power plants in the world, in which everyone can find out detailed and brief information about the NPP of interest. The project "Andromeda " is just developing, but it already gathers hundreds of thousands of readers and thousands of editors who complement the project.
